Our Vision and Mission

In ten years we hope to see girls in STEM as a convention by inspiring them to actively take an interest in STEM by creating mentorship, leadership, entrepreneurial and networking opportunities.We aim to extend our reach across the expanse of India, creating a diverse, inclusive community where everybody is heard.

Vision

To bridge the gender gap in S.T.E.M, by making it less intimidating, fun and accessible for young girls across India.

Mission

We will hold an annual innovation challenge which will serve to broaden horizons & allow girls to make smart, informed decisions about their careers.

Mentorship

Our Mentorship Program matches young girls with women working in the field of STEM to foster community, networking and support. This program is geared towards meeting the needs of students as they explore undergraduate opportunities and navigate their journey in STEM. We share information and resources about relevant questions, concerns or fears faced by girls in STEM

Our Initiatives:

Innovation Challenge



This challenge aims to give girls the opportunity to gain confidence and exposure to S.T.E.M based fields. It focuses on four major problem areas of healthcare, communication, environment and education. Each team will be tackling real-world issues and coming up with sustainable and feasible solutions. Girls are encouraged to have effective unique solutions, while honing their technical, teamwork and soft skills.

About us

Women in S.T.E.M jobs across India comprise a mere 14%. 38% of girls were less likely to take up a S.T.E.M career. The reason cited was that the field was male dominated and catered more to that gender, despite them performing well at S.T.E.M subjects at school. TrailblazHER set up in April 2020 by a mother-daughter duo with backgrounds in S.T.E.M aims to combat these issues and make women in S.T.E.M the new norm for India. At TrailblazHER girls are encouraged to take up STEM subjects at a younger age, along with mentorship and research opportunities which increase exposure to STEM. They also develop a wide range of abilities from technical to communication skills. We have S.T.E.M based curriculums designed by experts from leading colleges like M.I.T AND Harvard. Our Programme ensures that girls get hands-on experiential learning as well as proper engagement with material. We believe that our girls are the future of S.T.E.M in India.
